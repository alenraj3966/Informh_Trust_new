About Us

ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Our founder "karunaiyin sigaram" C. JOHNSON PAULRAJ was born on 26/06/1965 to MR. CHARLES SELVARAJ (Retd. pwd) and DAISY MARGRET (Retd. H.M Chennai Corp) in PURASAIVALKAM, CHENNAI. He completed his secondary education in Raja Muthaiya school, Mandaveli. Later he accomplished his Diploma in Printing Technology in SIGA -Chetpet.

He started his carrer in 1987 as a mentor for the visually challenged people in CHRISTIAN FOUNDATION FOR BLIND IN INDIA (CFBI) . Later in 1994 he started his very own ministry of serving the destitudes with God's vision and guidance. He still strives to stand for the upliftment of the people since 1994.

What We are

INFORMH (India Forum for the Mentally Handicapped) was established by C.Johnson Paulraj, Founder- President and a committee comprising of 5 trust members at Mahalakshmi Nagar, Guduvancherry in a rental home.

The humble opening with 4 inmates was inaugarated on 4th April,1994 by the District collector of Kanchipuram during that period Honourable Shri.Varaprasad Yadav.

Later, after 4 months we shifted to another rental home at EVP avenue, GST road, Vallancherry where we inhabited for next 5 years.

Finally in Jauary 31st 1999,we landed on Sri Rajeshwari nagar,vallancherry.The village people of vallancherry donated us the land of area 9600 sq.ft. that was a humble start under asbestos roof.

On our 15th anniversary ,SRM university's chairman Dr.Pachamuthu, provided us with a fully equipped concrete building of two floors with seperate wings for men and women with attached bathrooms.

At present there are 50 mentally ill patients being cared with love and affection, appropriate psychiatric treatment , three meals a day, cloths and comfortable shelter.
